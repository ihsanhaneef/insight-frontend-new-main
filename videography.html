GUIDELINES

  • All participants must register before the competition starts. Late registrations will not be accepted.
  • The theme will be revealed on the spot, and participants must incorporate it into their videos
  • Each participant is allowed to submit only one entry.
  • Only mobile phone cameras are allowed,other types of cameras are not permitted.
  • Videos should not exceed a maximum duration of 5 minutes. Submissions exceeding this limit will be disqualified. .
  • Edited videos are acceptable for judgment. Participants are encouraged to showcase their editing skills within the specified guidelines.
  • Entries should not include the name or any identifiable information of the participant to ensure impartial judging.
  • Participants should not feature themselves in the video shots.

  • Videos must adhere to ethical standards and not contain any explicit or offensive material
  • Videos should be submitted in MP4 format only. Late submissions will not be considered.
  • Videos will be judged based on creativity, adherence to the theme, technical skills, and overall impact.
  • Any violation of the rules or unsportsmanlike conduct may result in disqualification.
  • The judges' decisions are final and binding. No appeals or disputes will be entertained.
  • Organizers reserve the right to modify event details if necessary and will communicate any changes to participants promptly.
